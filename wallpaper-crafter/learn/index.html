Learn how to create a wallpaper for wallpaper-crafter

General method (without blender)

!!! Images all have to be in the same resolution !!! Images needed:
  • background.png - The background of the wallpaper
  • players.png - The pose of the player without armor layer using this skin
  • mask_player.png - Mask of the pose of the player without armor layer (No player > black, player > white)
  • shading_players.png - Shadows of the player without armor layer (Blend mode multiplication)
  • players_armor.png - The pose of the player with armor layer using this skin
  • mask_armor.png - Mask of the pose of the player with armor layer (No player > black, player > white)
  • shading_armor.png - Shadows of the player with armor layer (Blend mode multiplication)
  • foreground.png - Foreground of the image, applied over the image (You can add a watermark here)
Go to this site, select the above mentioned files and right-click the image. Then save it as "preview.png"

Blender method

!!! Images all have to be in the same resolution !!! Download my modified version of the Mashed Potato Rig.
Steps:
    1. In properties window goto "Scene > Render Properties > Color Management and change "View Transform" to "Standart"
    2. In properties window goto "Scene > View Layer > Passes > Cryptomatte and enable Material
    1. Hide Teeth, Tongue and Pupils in the viewport and for camera
    2. Change "Only Shadows" in Player material to 1
    3. Fix dark spots by moving overlapping meshes
    4. Render and save as "background.png"
    5. Unhide Teeth, Tongue and Pupils (unless not using face)
    6. Change "Only Shadows" in Player material back to 0
    OR
    1. Hide Players
    2. Render and save as "background.png"
    3. Show Players
  3. In properties window goto "Scene > Render Properties > Film > Pixel Filter" and change "Type" to "Blackman-Harris" and change "Width" to 0.01px
    1. Change "Overlay" in Player material to 0
    2. Render and save as "players.png"
    3. Save cryptomatte of player in compositing tab as "mask_player.png"
    4. Change "Overlay" in Player material to 1
    5. Render and save as "players_armor.png"
    6. Save cryptomatte of player in compositing tab as "mask_armor.png"
    1. Change "Shading" in Player material to 1
    2. Change "Overlay" in Player material to 0
    3. Render and save as "shading_players.png"
    4. Change "Overlay" in Player material to 1
    5. Render and save as "shading_armor.png"
Go to this site, select the above mentioned files and right-click the image. Then save it as "preview.png"
